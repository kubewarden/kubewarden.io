Chimera Kube

chimera-kube gives you the flexibility and power of WebAssembly (Wasm) to write and distribute admission and mutation webhooks – as easy as it gets.

The advantages of WebAssembly

Why use WebAssembly?

WebAssembly (abbreviated Wasm) is a portable compilation target for programming languages.

Freedom of choice

Write policies using your favorite programming language, as long as it can produce Wasm modules.

Feel at home

Policies are regular programs. Write them with your favorite tools, reuse your skills, libraries and best practices.

Portable

Wasm modules are portable, once built they can run on any kind of architecture and Operating System.
